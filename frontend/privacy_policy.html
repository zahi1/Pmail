Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pmail. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our platform and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect

We collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Data: includes first name, last name, company name, profile picture
  • Contact Data: includes email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Profile Data: includes your birthdate, credentials, employment history
  • Usage Data: includes information about how you use our platform

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide our messaging services
  • To facilitate job applications and hiring processes
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To communicate important notices and updates

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees and third parties who have a business need to know.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data including the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, or to object to processing.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
