Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pmail. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our system.

2. The Data We Collect

We collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Data: includes first name, last name, company name, profile picture
  • Contact Data: includes email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Profile Data: includes your birthdate, credentials, employment history
  • Usage Data: includes information about how you use our system

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide our messaging services
  • To facilitate job applications and hiring processes
  • To improve our system and user experience
  • To communicate important updates

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being used or accessed in an unauthorized way.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, or to object to processing.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will be posting the new privacy policy on this page, so check the "Last Updated" date.

For Recruiters

Application Status Update Policy

At Pmail, we are committed to providing a responsive and transparent experience for all users. To ensure timely communication between recruiters and job seekers, we have implemented the following policy regarding application status updates:

Mandatory Status Update Timeline

Recruiters and employers are required to update the status of each job application within 7 days of submission.

Warning System

First Violation: If you fail to update the status of an application within 7 days of submission, you will receive a warning message upon your next login.

Account Suspension

Second Violation: If you fail to update application statuses within the required timeframe a second time, your account will be automatically suspended.

This policy has been implemented to ensure that all job applicants receive updates fairly on their application status and to maintain the quality of communication within the Pmail system.
